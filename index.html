About

FinPixel Games is an indie game studio from Finland, founded with a simple mission: to create high-quality games and digital tools — from Finland to the world.

We are an AI-native studio, meaning artificial intelligence is integrated into every step of our workflow, from game design and development to art and audio. This allows us to move fast and punch above our weight, delivering polished experiences that would traditionally require a much larger team.

Our focus is on mobile games that are fun, accessible, and built with care. We believe in quality over quantity — every product we release is something we'd want to play ourselves.

Our first title, Beyond Orbit, is a space adventure coming soon to Google Play.
