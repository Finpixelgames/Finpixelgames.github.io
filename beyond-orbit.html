About the Game

Blast off into a procedurally generated universe where no two journeys are the same. Pilot your ship through an endless cosmos filled with planets, asteroids, and nebulae — all waiting to be explored.

Features
🌌

Explore the Universe

Fly freely through a vast, procedurally generated galaxy. Discover unique planets with rings and moons, drift through colorful nebulae, and dodge deadly asteroid fields.

🚀

Upgrade Your Ship

Choose from 10 unique ships, from nimble corvettes to powerful frigates. Upgrade weapons, shields, engines, and hull. Customize your ship's colors.

💥

4 Powerful Weapons

Machine Gun, Missiles, Laser Beam, and Planet Destroyer — each with 6 upgrade levels.

⚔️

Fight or Trade

Battle enemy fighters and epic bosses, or dock with trade ships to buy upgrades. Accept delivery and combat missions for extra credits.

🏆

Daily Quests & Achievements

Complete daily challenges and unlock 27 achievements across combat, exploration, economy, and survival.
