Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Introduction

FinPixel Games ("we", "our", "us") is a Finnish game studio that develops mobile games and digital tools. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our applications.

This policy applies to all applications published by FinPixel Games on Google Play and the App Store.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information We Do NOT Collect

Our applications do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as:

  • Name, email address, or phone number
  • Location data
  • Photos, contacts, or files from your device
  • Unique device identifiers for tracking purposes

2.2 Locally Stored Data

Our applications may store game progress, settings, and preferences locally on your device. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not accessible to us
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the application

2.3 Purchase Information

If you make in-app purchases, the transaction is processed entirely by Google Play or Apple App Store. We do not receive, process, or store any payment information (such as credit card numbers). We may receive a confirmation that a purchase was made, but no personal financial data.

2.4 Automatically Collected Data by Platform Services

Google Play and Apple App Store may automatically collect certain data such as:

  • Crash reports and performance data
  • Basic analytics (install counts, active users, device types)
  • Purchase history related to our applications

This data is collected by Google/Apple under their own privacy policies, not by us. We may access aggregated, anonymized statistics through Google Play Console or App Store Connect to improve our applications.

We may integrate additional third-party services in future updates, such as:

  • Social features (e.g., Google Play Games Services) for leaderboards and achievements

If and when such services are added, this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly, and users will be notified through an application update.

4. Children's Privacy

Our applications are not specifically directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Since our applications do not collect any personal information from any user, no special data collection from children occurs.

5. Data Security

Since we do not collect or transmit personal data, there is minimal data security risk from our applications. Game data stored locally on your device is protected by your device's own security features (screen lock, encryption, etc.).

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

As we are based in Finland (EU), the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) applies. Since we do not collect personal data, most GDPR rights are not applicable in practice. However, you have the right to:

  • Request information about any data we may hold about you
  • Request deletion of any data (local game data can be deleted by uninstalling the app)
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions

7. Data Retention

We do not retain any personal data. Locally stored game data remains on your device until you uninstall the application or clear the application's data through your device settings.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Significant changes will be communicated through application updates.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
